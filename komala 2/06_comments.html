A front-end developer is a type of developer who specializes in building the user-facing parts of websites and applications. They translate design mockups into functional code, focusing on the visual layout, styling, and interactivity that users experience. Key technologies include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are used to structure content, apply visual styles, and add dynamic behaviors. Essentially, they are responsible for the "front end" of a digital product, ensuring it is visually appealing, easy to navigate, and functions correctly.
